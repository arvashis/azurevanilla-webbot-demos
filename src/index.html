Tier 1 Full-Time Academy (FTA)

Tier 1 is the only Academy in the Pacific Northwest that has a full time training program. Athletes train full time Monday through Friday with a dedicated coaching staff to reach their potential.

Tier 1 FTA includes unlimited tennis training, strength & conditioning, Mental skills training, and Post training recovery.

Athletes have access to a dedicated 3000 square foot house made just for them. The FTA house is a safe-space for kids to do their home work on site during the training day.

Expert Coaches: Guiding You Every Step of the Way

At Tier 1 Performance Tennis Academy, our coaches are mentors and strategists, deeply invested in your tennis journey.

Our team combines diverse expertise, from technical skill to strategic insight, dedicated to unlocking each player's full potential. They offer personalized coaching, adapting to each player's strengths and goals.

Constantly evolving, our coaches integrate the latest in sports science and techniques. Beyond technical training, they support players with mental conditioning, nutrition, and career advice.

Join us for coaching that transforms your potential into excellence.

Dedicated facilities
in the heart of the Northwest

Hone your skills at our dedicated tennis facilities, nestled in the scenic heart of the Northwest in Woodinville Washington. Our academy boasts a selection of pristine indoor and outdoor courts, ensuring optimal training conditions all year round.

Complementing the courts is our expansive gym, designed for the specific needs of tennis athletes, enabling a complete fitness regimen. It's here amidst the inspiring landscapes of the Northwest that you'll find the perfect setting to push your limits and expand your game.
